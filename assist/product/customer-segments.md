# Customer Segments

## Purpose

CODEXSUN must serve different customer sizes without forcing every customer into enterprise complexity.

The product should feel light for small businesses and strong for larger enterprises.

## Segment 1: Starter Business

Typical needs:

- Basic billing.
- Customers and items.
- Simple accounting.
- GST invoice.
- Basic reports.
- Few users.
- Low customization.

Product behavior:

- Simple onboarding.
- Minimal settings.
- Default roles.
- Guided setup.
- Clean dashboard.

## Segment 2: Growing Business

Typical needs:

- Multiple users.
- Inventory.
- Purchase and sales.
- Branch or warehouse support.
- WhatsApp or mail integration.
- Role permissions.
- Better reports.

Product behavior:

- Feature activation.
- Configurable workflows.
- Module-based pricing.
- Industry pack selection.

## Segment 3: Industry-Specific Business

Typical needs:

- Garments, uPVC, printing, POS, software, or other industry behavior.
- Custom documents.
- Industry reports.
- Special billing and production workflows.
- Offline support where needed.

Product behavior:

- Industry pack activation.
- Templates and defaults.
- Industry-specific dashboards.
- Module boundaries preserved.

## Segment 4: Enterprise Tenant

Typical needs:

- Multi-company.
- Multi-branch.
- Dedicated database.
- High-volume transactions.
- Formal approvals.
- Strong audit.
- Advanced permissions.
- Dedicated support.
- Backup and restore process.
- Integration monitoring.

Product behavior:

- Enterprise configuration.
- Support console.
- Advanced security.
- Performance tuning.
- Optional dedicated containers.

## Segment Rule

Do not design the whole UI for the largest customer only.

Use progressive disclosure:

- Simple defaults.
- Advanced settings hidden until needed.
- Module activation based on subscription.
- Industry templates for faster setup.
- Enterprise controls available when enabled.
